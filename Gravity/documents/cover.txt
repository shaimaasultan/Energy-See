 appearing invisible to the human eye.
 
 Induction is a Phase Shift: 
 By moving a magnet, we introduce a Time-Based Phase Shift ($t$). 
 
 This breaks the cancellation and "frees" the energy into a measurable state (Electricity/Light).
 
 Gravity is a Grid Deformation: 
 Gravity is not a separate force but a "scrounging" of the spatial grid. 
 Near massive objects, space scrunches together, concentrating the energy density and bending 
 the path of light.
 
 Universal Rotation: 
 Stable systems (like Earth) and chaotic systems (like the Sun) 
 use rotation to organize and flip their "locked" energy, preventing 
 the universe from settling into a dead, inactive state.
